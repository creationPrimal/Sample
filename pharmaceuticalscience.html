A qualified student to study this one year course must have completed Ordinary level of Secondary education (Form 4 in Tanzania or translated results if studied outside Tanzania) Holders of Certificate of Secondary Education Examination (CSEE) with four passes in non-religious subject including ‘D’ passes in Chemistry and Biology.
